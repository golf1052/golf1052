Signal Windows Privacy Policy


Note that this Signal Private Messenger app is not an official Signal client. This app was not created by Signal Messenger, LLC and is not maintained by Signal Messenger, LLC. Our client however does connect you to the official Signal servers and allow you to message other Signal users.

When you sign up in the app you are signing up for a Signal account on the Signal service. The creators of this app are unable to delete your account with Signal.

Your phone number is only used to sign up for a Signal account.

On your device we store your Signal contacts and conversations. The creators of this app have no access to data stored on your device and no data from your device is sent to the creators of this app. We only send and receive data from the Signal servers, we do not transmit data to any other party.

To learn more about Signal's specific privacy policy please see their website at https://signal.org/legal/
