Twixel Privacy Policy

Twixel only stores and syncs a Twitch access token between your devices.

Twixel does not store any other Twitch information.

Twixel only has access to the information that you have allowed Twixel to view when you log into the app.

You can revoke these privileges from Twixel at any time by visiting this page on Twitch and selecting disconnect next to Twixel.
